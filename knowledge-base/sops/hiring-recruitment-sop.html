Hiring & Recruitment SOP Published v1

Process Owner: Angela Tino, Executive Operations Lead

Last Updated: July 22, 2026 · Review Cadence: Quarterly, or upon company growth beyond current team size

Purpose

This SOP documents how Canopi identifies, screens, interviews, and hires new team members across the Engineering, Product, and Executive teams. It exists to ensure a consistent, repeatable hiring process as the company grows beyond its current small team.

Scope

Covers the full hiring lifecycle from job posting through offer and confirmation of start date. Onboarding and equipment setup once a candidate accepts is covered separately in the New Hire Onboarding SOP.

Process Flow (Summary)

  1. Requesting Team Head identifies a hiring need and requests approval to hire
  2. Bionka Randolph or Angela Tino posts the opening on LinkedIn
  3. Bionka Randolph or Angela Tino screens incoming candidates
  4. Angela Tino shares the shortlist of candidates with Bionka Randolph
  5. Angela Tino emails qualified candidates to confirm availability and interest
  6. Angela Tino schedules 1st round interview with Bionka Randolph
  7. Bionka Randolph conducts 1st round interview
  8. If Bionka approves → candidate is referred to 2nd round with the relevant Team Head (Jagun Rimes for engineering, Dixon Lin for product) or a full panel if needed
  9. Angela Tino schedules the 2nd round interview
  10. Team feedback is collected and shared with Bionka Randolph
  11. Bionka Randolph makes the final hiring decision
  12. Bionka Randolph or Angela Tino informs the candidate and requests required items from them (ID, etc.)
  13. Bionka Randolph confirms start date to Angela Tino → triggers Onboarding SOP

Detailed Steps

Step 1: Identify Hiring Need

Who: Requesting Team Head
How: Team Head identifies a hiring need and requests approval to open the role.
Output: Approved role ready to post.

Step 2: Job Posting

Who: Bionka Randolph or Angela Tino
How: Post the open role on the company LinkedIn account with role title, department, and key requirements.
Output: Live job posting generating candidate applications.

Step 3: Candidate Screening

Who: Bionka Randolph or Angela Tino
When: As applications come in via LinkedIn
How: Review incoming applications/resumes against the role requirements to shortlist qualified candidates.
Output: Shortlist of candidates.

Step 4: Share Shortlist

Who: Angela Tino
How: Angela informs Bionka of the shortlisted candidates.
Output: Bionka and Angela aligned on which candidates to contact.

Step 5: Candidate Outreach

Who: Angela Tino
How: Email shortlisted candidates directly to confirm availability and continued interest in the role.
Output: Confirmed availability from interested candidates.

Step 6: Schedule 1st Round Interview

Who: Angela Tino
How: Schedule the candidate for a 1st round interview with Bionka Randolph.
Output: Interview scheduled and confirmed with candidate and Bionka.

Step 7: 1st Round Interview

Who: Bionka Randolph
How: Bionka conducts the initial interview. If she approves the candidate, they move to a 2nd round with the relevant Team Head or a panel.
Output: Go / no-go decision to proceed to 2nd round.

Step 8: 2nd Round Interview

Who: Relevant Team Head (Jagun Rimes for Engineering roles, Dixon Lin for Product roles) or full team panel if required
How: Team-specific interview to assess role fit. Whether this is a single interviewer or full panel is currently a judgment call based on role and team need (not yet formalized).
Output: Team feedback shared back with Bionka Randolph.

Step 9: Schedule 2nd Round Interview

Who: Angela Tino
How: Angela coordinates and schedules the 2nd round interview with the relevant Team Head or panel.
Output: 2nd round interview scheduled and confirmed.

Step 10: Feedback Collection

Who: Interviewing Team Head(s)/Panel → Bionka Randolph
How: Team feedback from the 2nd round is collected and shared with Bionka.
Output: Feedback ready to inform final hiring decision.

Step 11: Hiring Decision

Who: Bionka Randolph
How: Bionka reviews interview feedback and makes the final hiring decision.
Output: Final go/no-go decision on the candidate.

Step 12: Offer & Document Collection

Who: Bionka Randolph or Angela Tino
How: Inform the candidate they've been selected and request required documents from them (e.g. ID).
Output: Candidate accepts offer and submits required documents.

Step 13: Start Date Handoff

Who: Bionka Randolph → Angela Tino
How: Bionka informs Angela of the confirmed start date, which triggers the New Hire Onboarding SOP.
Output: Onboarding process begins.

Exceptions and Edge Cases

ScenarioWhat to Do
Panel vs. single interviewer needed for 2nd roundJudgment call by Bionka and/or the relevant Team Head based on role seniority and team fit needs. Not yet formalized — revisit as hiring volume grows.
Requesting Team Head is not the final decision-makerThe Team Head (Jagun Rimes for engineering, Dixon Lin for product) provides technical/team-fit input, but Bionka Randolph makes the final hiring call in all cases.
Candidate cannot make scheduled interview timeAngela Tino re-coordinates availability directly with the candidate and updates the relevant interviewer(s).
Role needed is outside Engineering or Product (e.g. future Executive/Admin hire)Process defaults to Bionka Randolph handling both rounds directly, with Angela Tino supporting screening and scheduling as usual.
