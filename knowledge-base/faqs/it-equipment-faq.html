IT & Equipment FAQ Published v1

Download original Word document

Prepared By: Angela Tino, Executive Operations Lead

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

For: Anyone with a question about equipment, tools, or access at Canopi

Quick answers to the IT and equipment questions that come up most. Every answer links to the full policy or SOP if you want more detail.

Getting Equipment

Q: What equipment do I get when I start?

A: Standard equipment is provisioned as part of the New Hire Onboarding & Equipment Setup SOP. Team-specific equipment needs (e.g. for Engineering or Product) aren't fully defined yet — that SOP flags it as an open item to confirm with Jagun and Dixon.

Q: My equipment is broken, or I need a replacement — who do I contact?

A: Bionka Randolph handles equipment directly.

Getting Access to Tools

Q: I need access to a tool I don't currently have. What do I do?

A: Post in the #access-requests Slack channel with the tool name and your reason for needing it. See the IT/System Access Request SOP for the full process.

Q: Who actually approves my request?

A: It depends on the tool. Company-wide, admin, or financial tools go through Bionka Randolph; engineering tools go through Jagun Rimes; product/design tools go through Dixon Lin. The SOP's Tool Category Reference table has the full breakdown.

Q: How quickly should I expect a response?

A: There's no fixed turnaround time, but if something's genuinely urgent, say so explicitly in your request — approvers aim to respond same-day for flagged urgent requests.

Q: Is there a list of every tool Canopi currently uses?

A: This is still being built out under Tools & Systems > Internal Applications in the repository. If you don't see a tool listed there yet, that's a gap worth flagging, not a sign it's unauthorized.

Security

Q: Do I need to use a password manager?

A: It's strongly recommended, though not mandatory yet. At minimum, use a unique, strong password for every company account.

Q: Should I turn on two-factor authentication?

A: Yes, wherever it's available — especially for anything touching customer data, code, or financial systems.

Q: I think my account or device may be compromised, or I've lost my device. What do I do?

A: Tell Bionka Randolph immediately. Speed matters more than certainty here — don't wait until you're sure.

Personal Devices

Q: Can I check Slack or email from my personal phone?

A: Yes — just keep the device secured with a passcode or biometric lock, and don't let others use it while you're logged into a company account. When you eventually leave Canopi, only the company accounts are removed from your device, not the device itself.

Software & Installation

Q: Can I install whatever software I want on my company laptop?

A: No — check with Bionka Randolph or your Team Head first, especially for anything that would access company data. See the Acceptable Use Policy.

Offboarding

Q: What happens to my equipment and access when I leave Canopi?

A: Equipment is returned and general access (Slack, email, company-wide tools) is revoked by Bionka. If you're on Engineering or Product, Jagun or Dixon additionally revokes your team-specific tool access. See the Offboarding SOP.

Who Do I Contact
